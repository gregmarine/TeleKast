Segment
Segment (seg-m&nt) a : a separate piece of something : BIT, FRAGMENT <chop the stalks into short segments> b : one of the constituent parts into which a body, entity, or quantity is divided or marked off by or as if by natural boundaries <all segments of the population agree>

A segment, in TeleKast, is one piece of a script. Segments make up the script and can be edited individually. Furthermore, you can rearrange segments within the script. Segments are made up of a Title, Text to be used and Camera, Audio, Video, Talent and Other cues. Segments give substance to the script.
