Welcome to TeleKast Help
Welcome to TeleKast Help: Your place to learn how TeleKast can help you! To display information about TeleKast in this window, click topics in the Contents sidebar. Click the plus icon next to a topic to see its subtopics.

You can look at TeleKast in two ways. First of all, it is a script editor. The scripts you create can be used for feature stories, interviews and, of course, for the teleprompter. The teleprompter is the second way you can look at TeleKast. In short, you don't have to memorize your lines. You simply read what the teleprompter says and you will look like a pro.

It is the hope of the TeleKast community that you will enjoy using this software. Please provide feedback as you use it and TeleKast will continue to become a better application to meet your needs.

Note: This software is in an alpha state. The largest effort is going toward finding and fixing bugs. New features will not be added to the 1.0.0.* series of releases. We hope to be in a beta state very soon. And soon after that, a production quality version will be released.
